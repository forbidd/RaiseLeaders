About Raise Leaders

Responsive, Minimalism

Raise Leaders Martial Arts was founded in 1989 and has been serving South Florida for over two decades. Our 5,000 square foot facility serves an average of 300 families per year. Our programs include youth and adult martial arts classes at our studio and on zoom, an after-care program, leadership program and day and summer camps. Students are enrolled in our Tigers program as young as 3 years old and our adult program can be adapted for people of all skill and fitness levels. At the core of RLMA is our leadership program, which emphasizes personal responsibility and service to others.
